METTLE Project

Revolutionizing Energy Access in Africa

£1.4M Innovate UK funded project developing high-capacity flexible energy storage systems for Sub-Saharan Africa.

Project Features

Second Life EV Batteries

Sustainable energy storage using repurposed electric vehicle batteries.

Strategic Partnerships

Collaboration with In2tec, UoL, and Nevadic Solar for maximum impact.

Advanced BMS

Innovative Battery Management System with remote monitoring capabilities.

Sustainable Solutions

Reducing reliance on diesel generators with clean energy alternatives.

Project Impact
